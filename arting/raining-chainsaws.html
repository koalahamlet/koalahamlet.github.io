Raining Chainsaws

Raining Chainsaws is a puppet and theater troupe based in San Francisco California. They’ve produced a variety of shows throughout the years. From shadow puppets and marionettes, to full body costumes and surrealist parades. Myko Huff has been a member of RC’s Board of Directors since 2022 and assisted in puppet production, organization, and performance for several shows.
